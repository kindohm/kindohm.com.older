--- layout: post title: "The Engage Event - Kinect and more" ---

On September 14th at 4:00 PM, Avtex will be co-hosting an event called Engage. It will be held at the Microsoft Technology Center in Minneapolis and will feature a variety of unique applications that my team and I at Avtex have developed - six of which leverage the Kinect sensor.

The Engage event is a unique experiential event designed to educate marketing, customer experience and technology professionals about new and unique customer engagement methods leveraging emerging technologies.

The event will focus on creating unique in-person experiences by combining engaging physical displays from nParallel with technology solutions from Avtex.

Join us for wine and appetizers and to see how the physical and digital worlds come together to help you better connect with your customers.

If you're interested in seeing what we've built, you can register today at www.engageevent.net.
