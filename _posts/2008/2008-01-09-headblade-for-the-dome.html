--- layout: post title: "HeadBlade for the Dome" ---

I picked up a HeadBlade last night.

Another guy at work recommended one, suggesting it was really fast to use rather than a regular razor. I hate shaving my head in the morning because it is time consuming. I thought I'd try a HeadBlade since they're only $10.

I tried it out last night, and it was kind of strange to use at first. I'll see how fast it really is to use after I get more familiar with using it. But otherwise, it worked really well. I have a nice, slick dome this morning.

From the HeadBlade FAQ:

"I can't read. Do you have an illustration that shows how to use this smart little invention?"

I like funny marketing people.
