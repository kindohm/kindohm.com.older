--- layout: post title: "Daddy, why is electrical work so hard?" ---

Here's a word of advice out there for all you do-it-yourself'ers who are doing your own electrical work at home: make sure that if you're testing a circuit with a light on it that the light bulb isn't burned out.

I estimate that you will save about 60 minutes of work if you take this advice.
