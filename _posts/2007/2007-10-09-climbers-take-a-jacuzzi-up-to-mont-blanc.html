--- layout: post title: "Climbers take a jacuzzi up to Mont Blanc" ---

Climbers carry a portable jacuzzi up to the top of Mont Blanc, the tallest mountain in the Alps. They actually use it at the top, but I didn't see anyone jumping out and rolling around in the snow. I guess they had to draw the line somewhere!
