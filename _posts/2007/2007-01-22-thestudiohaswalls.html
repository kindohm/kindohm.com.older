This is what we did on Sunday:

We're about 99% done with the studio sheetrock. I can count on one hand how many small pieces I have left to put up. I wanted my helpers to focus on the big pieces that I could get done on my own. All I have left are a few things around the electrical panel, one piece above the window, and one over the doorway. I should be able to get those done in an hour - maybe two if I'm slow :)

I'm so glad that we made great progress yesterday, and it was outstanding to have four other people helping with the work.

Looking back, I think it's really funny that I thought I could get that room done in a day. Instead it took two days of more than eight hours each.

And of course, it's fun to imagine what it'll be like to make music in the room now that the walls have given it some shape.
