--- layout: post title: "R.I.P. Max Roach" ---

Max Roach dies at age 83

Max Roach was one of the few drummers that I really ever considered one of my favorites. He was the first drummer I ever truly studied, and I transcribed a few of his solos back when I took drum set lessons. I also played his Blues for Big Sid drum solo. I can't say I kept up with anything he had done recently, but to this day I listen to his old stuff.
