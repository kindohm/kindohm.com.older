--- layout: post title: "A tale of two blogs" ---

I've started blogging on Inetium's blog server (http://blogs.inetium.com/blogs/mhodnick, RSS). I see value in blogging for Inetium, but I don't want to give up posting technical content on my personal site either. I also want to avoid cross-posting because I want both "blogs" to be as valuable as they can on their own.

Thus, my plan is to use my Inetium blog for a narrower scope of topics such as WinFX and WPF and any other work-related content. I'll keep using my personal site for any personal development projects outside of work (not that I have any right now) and non-work-related content.
