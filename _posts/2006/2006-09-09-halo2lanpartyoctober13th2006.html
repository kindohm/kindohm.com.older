Date and Time

10/13/2006. We'll be starting at 5:00 and ending around whenever.

Directions

2396 Molnau Ct.
Chaska, MN 55318
Click here for Google map

There's a chance that the Google map (and other map services isn't reliable. So use it at your own risk. Here are some alternate directions:

From 494
  1. From 494, Take Hwy 5 West
  2. Turn left (south) on Hwy 41
  3. Turn right (west) on Hundertmark Rd.
  4. After the single stop sign on Hundertmark, you'll enter a round-about with a Washington Monument sculpture.
  5. Go 3/4 around the round-about and turn out of it so that you're heading south on Clover Ridge Drive
  6. Go past the school on your left
  7. Take the first left onto Molnau Ct.
  8. 2nd house on the left
From Burnsville/south metro
  1. From 35W, take Hwy 13 south toward Shakopee
  2. Exit/veer onto 169 South, toward Mankato
  3. Go north on Hwy 41.
  4. Turn left (west) on Hundertmark Rd.
  5. Follow the directions above from Hundertmark
Food

Please plan on brining a little cash for ordering pizza. If you'd like something special to drink, please bring your own beverage. Bringing stuff to share for everyone is great too.
