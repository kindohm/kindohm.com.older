Nikki and I watched Heat (Pacino, Di Nero) this weekend. I thought it was pretty good, but what I really liked about it was the shootout scene in downtown L.A. I liked it because it wasn't fluffed and overdone like a typical Hollywood action movie, but severely loud and violent. No music, no big explosions. Just automatic rifles and it was f*cking loud and scary. I'd recommend the movie just for that scene.
