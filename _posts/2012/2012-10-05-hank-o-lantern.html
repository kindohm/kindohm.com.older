--- layout: post title: "Make a Hank-O'-Lantern" ---

This fall season, stop carving Jack-O'-Lanterns. For far too long, pumpkins have dominated Halloween with their slimy, seedy guts. This year, there's a new tradition: Hank-O'-Lanterns.

What is a Hank-O'-Lantern? A Hank-O'-Lantern is a hollowed apple with a carved Halloween face.

Benefits of a Hank-O'-Lantern:

  • They take a fraction of the time to make compared to a Jack-O'-Lantern
  • You can eat the fruit as you hollow it out and carve it
  • No slimy, gooey, seedy mess
  • They aren't Jack-O'-Lanterns
