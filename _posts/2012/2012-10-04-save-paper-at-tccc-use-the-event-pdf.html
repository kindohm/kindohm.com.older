For this weekend's Twin Cities Code Camp at the University of Minnesota, we are limiting the number of paper copies of the event schedule and event details. If you have a smart phone or tablet, please use your device to download and/or view the online PDF version of the schedule and other event details. You can find the PDF here: twincitiescodecamp.com/TCCC/Fall2012/TCCC13Booklet.pdf

We also have an HTML view of the schedule: twincitiescodecamp.com/TCCC/Fall2012/Schedule.aspx

If you do not have a smart phone or tablet, or if you prefer a printed copy, please plan on printing out your own copy before you arrive at the event.
