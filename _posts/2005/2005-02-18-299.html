--- layout: post title: "DnB artist recommendation" ---

If you're into the Drum n Bass musical genre, then check out RAkoon [^].  Some of the darkest and most creative DnB I've heard in a while.  Check out Therak and EVOL for a start.  He uses some Star Wars (”He's more machine now than man”) and Lord of the Rings (”You shall not pass!!”) samples in a few tracks...  A lot of producers try and force movie samples like those into tracks and it sounds cheesey, but I think this guy does it creatively while making them fit in with the rest of the music. 

Enjoy.
