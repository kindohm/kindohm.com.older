--- layout: post title: "What's in my car stereo - April edition" ---
  • The Melvins, Lustmord - Pigs of the Roman Empire. I'm a huge Melvins fan, and this album does not disappoint. I picked this one up last Friday. Some classic Melvins sounds plus a lot of ambient compositions as well.
  • Meshuggah - Nothing. Math metal, progressive metal, whatever. They're from Sweden, and they rock. A brain-spilling experience. I dare anyone to fit more polyrhythms, hemiolas, and time signature changes into 60 minutes.
  • Radiohead - Kid A.
  • Radiohead - Amnesiac. I got into a Radiohead mode after Neil exposed me to a group called Hard 'n Phirm and their "Rodeohead" track.
  • 311 - Evolver.
  • Porno for Pyros - Porno for Pyros. I listened to this a lot in high school, and just picked it up on CD on Friday. Very original drumming by Stephen Perkins.
  • Mudvayne - L.D. 50. More math, progressive hardcore. I haven't picked up Mudvayne's newest yet, but this debut release is their best so far.
