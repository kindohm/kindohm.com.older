--- layout: post title: "I walked the line" ---

Nikki and I saw Walk the Line on Friday.  Not too bad, except that it was pretty much the same movie as Ray.  Maybe it was the unfortunate and coincidental timing of each artists' death, or maybe it was Hollywood just trying to repeat the success of another hit movie.  But Walk the Line seemed like the same story told the same way about a lost musical legend - just a different musical genre. 

The acting was excellent.  Phoenix was spectacular.  I'd recommend seeing it, but if you didn't like Ray then you might want to think twice.
